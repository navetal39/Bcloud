Sign Up

Here you can sign up to our service. After you sign up, you need to download our program and sign in to your user from the program.

Important: You must download and install 'python 2.7' if you want to use our product. You can download 'python 2.7' from here.

Instructions:

  1. Sign up here. Be aware the the following characters are forbidden for use: '|','\', '/', ':', '*', '?', '"', '<', '>'. Furthermore, your username and your password must be at least 4 characters long, and cannot be greater then 31 in length.
  2. If you haven't installed 'python 2.7' yet - do it now according to the text in red that is seen above. If you have - proceed to the next step.
  3. Download the Bcloud installer from the downloads page.
  4. Run the Cloud installer and follow the instructions (of course not these, the instructions that are in it).
  5. After you install Bcloud choose the 'run' or 'run.bat' file and run it. Then log in to your account and only then your synced folder will be created.
  6. Enjoy our product and stay in touch!
